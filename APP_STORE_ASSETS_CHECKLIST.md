## iOS App Store

### Required Assets

#### App Icon
- **Size:** 1024x1024 pixels
- **Format:** PNG (no transparency)
- **Location:** `./assets/icon.png`
- **Requirements:**
  - No rounded corners (Apple adds them automatically)
  - No alpha channel
  - No drop shadows or effects
  - High quality, sharp image

## Google Play Store

### Required Assets

#### App Icon
- **Size:** 512 x 512 pixels
- **Format:** PNG (32-bit with alpha channel)
- **Location:** `./assets/icon.png`
- **Requirements:**
  - Square image
  - No rounded corners (Google adds them)
  - High quality, sharp image

#### Feature Graphic
- **Size:** 1024 x 500 pixels
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Location:** `./assets/feature-graphic.png`
- **Requirements:**
  - Horizontal banner
  - Appears at top of store listing
  - Showcase app branding

#### Screenshots (Phone)
- **Size:** Minimum 320px, maximum 3840px (width)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 9:16
- **Required:** Minimum 2 screenshots
- **Recommended:** 8 screenshots
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Requirements:**
  - At least 2 screenshots required
  - Maximum 8 screenshots
  - All must be same aspect ratio

#### Screenshots (Tablet) - Optional
- **Size:** Minimum 320px, maximum 3840px (width)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 9:16
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Requirements:**
  - Only if tablet-optimized
  - Same requirements as phone screenshots

#### App Preview Video (Optional)
- **Duration:** 30 seconds to 2 minutes
- **Format:** MP4, 3GP, or WebM
- **Resolution:** 1080p or higher
- **Requirements:**
  - Showcase app features
  - High quality
  - No audio narration required

## Asset Creation Guidelines

### Design Principles

1. **Consistency**
   - Use consistent branding across all assets
   - Maintain color scheme and typography
   - Follow brand guidelines

2. **Quality**
   - Use high-resolution images
   - Ensure sharp, clear graphics
   - Avoid pixelation or blur

3. **Clarity**
   - Show key features clearly
   - Use readable text
   - Avoid clutter

4. **Appeal**
   - Make screenshots visually appealing
   - Highlight best features
   - Use engaging visuals

### Screenshot Best Practices

1. **Show Key Features**
   - Home screen
   - AI Predictions screen
   - Paper Trading screen
   - Analytics dashboard
   - Settings screen

2. **Add Text Overlays**
   - Feature names
   - Benefits
   - Call-to-action text

3. **Use Device Frames**
   - Show app in device context
   - Professional appearance
   - Better visual appeal

4. **Maintain Consistency**
   - Same style across all screenshots
   - Consistent color scheme
   - Unified design language

### App Icon Guidelines

1. **Simplicity**
   - Simple, recognizable design
   - Works at small sizes
   - Avoid fine details

2. **Branding**
   - Reflects app identity
   - Memorable and unique
   - Professional appearance

3. **Colors**
   - Use brand colors
   - High contrast
   - Stands out in app store
